'BLACKJACK' GAME RULES

1) Start Game:- Selects two cards at random for starting the game
2) New Card:- Selects a Card at random
3) You win if the total of cards is 21
4) If you win the bid amount doubles
5) If you loose your bid amount reduces to 0.
6) You can not play with 0 bid amount, you need to RESTART GAME
